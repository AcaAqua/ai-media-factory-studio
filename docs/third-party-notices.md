# Third-Party Notices

AI Media Factory Studio is a local-first control tool. It can connect to or work with third-party software, models, workflows, APIs, and assets, but those materials are not bundled unless explicitly stated.

## ComfyUI

ComfyUI must be installed separately. Review the ComfyUI project license, documentation, custom-node requirements, and runtime configuration before use.

## Ollama

Ollama must be installed separately. Review Ollama's license, model handling, and local service configuration before use.

## Custom Nodes

ComfyUI custom nodes are distributed by their own authors or repositories. Users are responsible for checking each custom node's license, install instructions, update policy, and security implications.

## Models and Related Files

Model files, LoRA files, VAE files, ControlNet files, embeddings, and upscalers may each have separate licenses, usage restrictions, content policies, and commercial-use conditions. Users are responsible for reviewing the terms for every file they download or import.

## Workflows and Prompts

External workflows, prompt packs, and examples may have their own licenses or usage terms. Check the source, author, distribution page, and any included license text before reuse or redistribution.

## External Services

Civitai, Hugging Face, GitHub, and other external services have their own terms of service, acceptable-use policies, content restrictions, and account rules. Users are responsible for complying with those service terms.

## No Third-Party Guarantee

AI Media Factory Studio does not guarantee the safety, legality, license status, quality, or availability of third-party software, models, workflows, APIs, or assets.
