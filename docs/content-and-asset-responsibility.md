# Content and Asset Responsibility

AI Media Factory Studio helps organize local creative operations. It does not determine rights, ownership, commercial usability, copyright status, or legal compliance.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for confirming rights and usage conditions for:

- images
- videos
- prompts
- workflows
- reference materials
- generated outputs
- imported assets
- third-party model and LoRA files

## Sensitive Asset Types

Take extra care when working with:

- real people or likenesses
- third-party copyrighted works
- trademarks and logos
- fictional characters
- client materials
- private or personal reference images
- platform-restricted content

## Commercial Use

Commercial use may depend on model licenses, LoRA licenses, source asset permissions, platform terms, and local laws. Review all applicable terms before publishing, selling, distributing, or delivering generated assets.

## Recommended Provenance Records

When importing external reference images, workflows, prompts, or model-related materials, keep records such as:

- source URL
- author or publisher
- acquisition date
- license or usage condition
- model or workflow version
- intended use

## Before Publication

Before publishing, selling, distributing, or sharing generated outputs, users should review the applicable rights, model terms, platform rules, and local legal requirements.
